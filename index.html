My Valorant Statistics

Valorant is a highly popular tactical first-person shooter (FPS) game developed and published by Riot Games. Released in June 2020, Valorant has quickly gained widespread acclaim for its engaging gameplay and unique blend of traditional FPS elements with character-based abilities. Set in a near-futuristic world, Valorant pits two teams of five players against each other, with one side playing as attackers and the other as defenders. The game revolves around the concept of planting and defusing a device called the Spike. The attacking team's objective is to plant the Spike at designated bomb sites, while the defending team aims to prevent the plant or defuse the Spike if it's planted.
